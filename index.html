About

Since 1999, Maya Driving School has trained thousands of confident drivers. The joyful reviews of our former students and the passionate dedication of our instructors are a testament to our quality.

Unlike other driving schools, we teach each aspect of the vehicle before touching the road. Our step-by-step method ensures a solid understanding of road rules, vehicle control, and real-world scenarios.

From reverse parking to narrow road handling and hill climbs, our expert-designed syllabus prepares you for any driving challenge — with the confidence to match.
