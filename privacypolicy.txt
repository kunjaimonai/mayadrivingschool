**Privacy Policy for Maya Driving School App**

**Effective Date: 24-06-2025 
**Version:** 2.1.0 (Build 14)

Maya Driving School ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide when using our mobile application.

---

### 1. Information We Collect

When you use the Maya Driving School app, we may collect the following information:

- **Personal Information**: Name, phone number, email address, and profile photo (optional).
- **Booking Information**: Selected dates, times, and booking preferences.
- **Device Information**: Basic device information such as model, operating system, and app version, for support and diagnostics.
- **Authentication Data**: Login credentials (stored securely) and session details.

---

### 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

- Enable student self-registration and account management.
- Facilitate and manage driving lesson bookings.
- Allow profile photo uploads for identification purposes.
- Enable admin review and approval of new student registrations.
- Improve our app’s functionality, performance, and user experience.
- Respond to user queries and provide customer support.

---

### 3. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

We may share your data only with:

- **Authorised school administrators** for managing bookings and user approvals.
- **Service providers** strictly as needed for app hosting or backend services (e.g., secure cloud storage).

---

### 4. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption, secure authentication, and regular monitoring to protect your data.

---

### 5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access and update your personal information.
- Request deletion of your account.
- Contact us with privacy-related questions or concerns.

Please email us at **\[[support@mayadrivingschool.in](mailto:support@mayadrivingschool.in)]** for any privacy-related requests.

---

### 6. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

### 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. If we make significant changes, we will notify users via the app or by email.
